About Karlo's Coffee Spot

More than just coffee, Karlo's Coffee Spot is a place where people connect, relax, and enjoy handcrafted beverages made with passion.

Since opening our doors in 2015, we have been committed to serving premium coffee made from ethically sourced beans. Every drink is carefully prepared by our experienced baristas, creating an inviting atmosphere for studying, working, or catching up with friends.
